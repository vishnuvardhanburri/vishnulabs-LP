# Recording Checklist

## Before recording
- Open all demo tabs and test every click path once.
- Close notifications, email popups, and private tabs.
- Zoom browser to 100% and use one consistent window size.
- Keep one clean tab title for each automation.
- Prepare your opening line and final CTA.

## Recording settings
- Resolution: 1920x1080.
- Frame rate: 30 fps.
- Cursor: visible with slight highlight.
- Webcam: optional; use top-right small circle if enabled.
- Audio: microphone gain set so voice peaks below clipping.

## On-camera structure (every video)
- 0:00 to 0:15: pain point.
- 0:15 to 1:30: workflow walkthrough.
- 1:30 to 2:10: results and business impact.
- 2:10 to 2:30: CTA to book a call.

## Editing pass
- Trim start and end dead air.
- Cut mistakes and long pauses.
- Add 2 to 4 text callouts max.
- Keep final duration under 3:00.
- Export mp4 at 1080p.

## Publish checklist
- Upload to YouTube unlisted or Vimeo.
- Add title and short description from `thumbnails-and-hooks.md`.
- Verify playback on mobile and desktop.
- Paste links into website section data.
