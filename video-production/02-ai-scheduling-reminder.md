# 02 - AI Scheduling & Reminder Assistant

## Goal
Prove reduced no-shows and faster booking.

## Duration
2:15 to 2:45

## Target audience
Lawyers, consultants, real estate agents.

## Scene flow
| Time | Screen Action | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00-0:12 | Show missed call / no-show problem text | "If bookings are manual, leads drop and no-shows increase." |
| 0:12-0:40 | Show chatbot collecting preferred time | "The assistant qualifies intent and offers real-time slots from your calendar." |
| 0:40-1:05 | Show Google Calendar or Calendly sync | "Once selected, it auto-books and blocks your calendar instantly." |
| 1:05-1:35 | Show SMS and email reminder messages | "It sends reminder sequences via SMS and email so attendance stays high." |
| 1:35-2:00 | Show reschedule/no-show recovery flow | "If someone misses, the bot triggers rebooking flow automatically." |
| 2:00-2:25 | Show business metrics card | "Result: less admin time, fewer missed meetings, and more closed opportunities." |

## End CTA
"Want this running on your calendar this week?"
