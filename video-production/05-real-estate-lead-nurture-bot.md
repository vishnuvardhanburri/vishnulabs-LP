# 05 - Real Estate Follow-up & Lead Nurture Bot

## Goal
Show reactivation of cold leads with automated drip follow-up.

## Duration
2:20 to 2:50

## Target audience
Solo real estate agents and small broker teams.

## Scene flow
| Time | Screen Action | Voiceover |
|---|---|---|
| 0:00-0:12 | Show stale leads list in CRM | "Most real estate teams have lead lists that never get consistent follow-up." |
| 0:12-0:45 | Show drip sequence setup (SMS + email) | "This bot runs personalized follow-ups automatically across text and email." |
| 0:45-1:15 | Show lead reply coming in | "When a lead replies, the system classifies intent and highlights hot prospects first." |
| 1:15-1:40 | Show routing to agent/CRM task | "Interested responses are pushed straight to the agent with context and next step." |
| 1:40-2:10 | Show meeting booking or callback trigger | "You can auto-trigger booking links or callback tasks for instant conversion." |
| 2:10-2:35 | Show outcome statement | "Result: more conversations from old leads and less manual chasing every day." |

## End CTA
"If you want your old lead database working again, this is the fastest setup."
